## Standardized test protocol (gives every post its E-E-A-T evidence)

For each tool, produce with the free tier:
1. A complete 45-minute lesson plan: 9th-grade science, "Introduction to Photosynthesis".
2. The same lesson differentiated for mixed-ability + an IEP accommodation.
3. A 10-question formative quiz aligned to the lesson objectives.
Record: time taken, edits needed before usable, factual errors, paywall walls hit, export options. Screenshots at each step.
