# Cluster 03 — Desk & Home-Office Gear Roundups

Track A fast-earning engine per [pinterest-product-roundups](../../kb/tactics/pinterest-product-roundups.md). Consumer products, mass demand, brand-coherent with SoloStack (gear for one-person businesses).

## First 4 roundup hub pages

| # | Page (target pin searches) | Products (proven-demand picks, researched at drafting) |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 7 Desk Upgrades Under $50 That Actually Matter | monitor riser, cable tray, desk pad, clamp lamp, footrest, headphone hook, laptop stand |
| 2 | The Small-Desk Home Office Setup (Under $150 Total) | compact setups for tight spaces — high Pinterest demand |
| 3 | Cable Management That Takes 10 Minutes | cheap, visual, extremely pinnable |
| 4 | The Ergonomic Starter Kit Nobody Overspends On | chair cushion, wrist rest, monitor arm, footrest |

Each page: verdict-first list, one honest line per product ("skip this if…"), disclosure above the list, `/go/`-style or tagged Amazon links once the account exists.

## Pin batch per page
3–4 pins per page from `make-pin.mjs` with varied angles/titles; descriptions with keyword + #affiliate tag, drafted by agent, pasted by owner.

## Sequencing (protects the Amazon 180-day clock)
1. Publish all 4 hub pages + ~15 pins linking to them (no Amazon links yet — pages list products with "check current price" placeholders).
2. Watch Pinterest outbound clicks for 2–3 weeks.
3. When pins are clicking (>50 outbound/week), owner signs up for Amazon Associates → links go live into already-flowing traffic → 3 sales/180 days becomes low-risk.

## Non-promotional 20% (account health)
Checklist/tip pins from the same generator: "10-minute desk reset checklist", "WFH posture checklist" — link to site, no affiliate content.
