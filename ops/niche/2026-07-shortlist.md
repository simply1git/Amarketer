# Niche Shortlist & Recommendation

## Method

Criteria weighted for our specific situation: solo operator, $0 budget, AI-agent-driven content, new site with zero authority in the post-Helpful-Content era. The 2026 SEO reality check shaped everything: generic affiliate content sites took 40–70% traffic losses; what still works is **deep micro-niche authority + genuine first-hand experience + multi-channel presence**.

**Our structural advantage:** the AI agent can sign up for free tiers of *software* products and produce genuine hands-on testing content (screenshots, workflows, real comparisons) at zero cost. That's real "Experience" for E-E-A-T — impossible for us with physical products (no budget to buy, nothing to photograph). **This alone eliminates physical-product niches** (senior care tech, smart home/energy — both otherwise attractive gaps).

## Recommendation: **A — AI tools for educators & students**

Working title: reviews, comparisons, and workflow guides for AI-powered teaching/learning tools (lesson-planning AI, quiz generators, writing feedback tools, study/note-taking apps, classroom management SaaS).

Why it wins:
1. **The competition gap is real.** Generic "best AI tools" listicles are saturated; almost nobody covers "does this AI quiz generator actually handle a 9th-grade chemistry syllabus" with hands-on depth. Vertical-specific AI content converts better and ranks against thinner competition.
2. **Every product is agent-testable free** — our E-E-A-T engine works at full power.
3. **Recurring SaaS commissions** (the 2026 sweet spot: 20–50% recurring) rather than one-time physical-product rates.
4. **Structural demand cycles** (back-to-school, exam seasons) give the tactics KB clean trigger-action patterns.
5. **Likely owner affinity** (education background) → credible author identity for E-E-A-T.

Runner-up: **C** if the owner has no education affinity — but it must be sliced to one micro-vertical (e.g., AI tools for freelance writers) to be viable.

Rejected: **B** (SERP competition suicidal for a zero-authority site despite the best commissions), senior-care tech and smart-home/energy (untestable physical products on $0 budget).
