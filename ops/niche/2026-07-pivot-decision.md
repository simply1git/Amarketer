# Pivot: Social-First Distribution, New Niche

## What the owner asked for
Earnings-oriented, social-platform affiliate model ("promote products with affiliate links on YouTube/Pinterest"), proven blueprints instead of from-scratch invention, no more planning questions — agent decides and completes.

## Decisions

1. **Niche: AI tools & software for one-person businesses / side income.**
   Rationale: highest accessible recurring commissions (40–60% recurring per earlier program research — Systeme.io 60% lifetime, GetResponse up to 60%); the audience is actively buying tools to make money, so affiliate CTR/conversion is structurally high; every product is software the agent can hands-on test free (our E-E-A-T engine); YouTube demand for "best AI tools" content is large and proven by many fast-growing faceless channels.
   Trade-off accepted: more competition than the education niche — offset by social-first distribution (algorithmic reach, not SERP rank) and proven-format modeling.

2. **Channel priority inverted: YouTube (faceless) primary, Pinterest secondary, site = link hub.**
   Many programs require a site for approval, and some platforms/programs restrict direct affiliate links — pins/videos link to hub pages carrying the affiliate links. Site SEO becomes a byproduct, not the strategy.

3. **Proven-blueprint method (legit version).** Model the formats of successful channels (topics, hooks, structures, what they promote); produce original scripts/visuals/testing. Never copy content itself — copyright strikes + platform duplicate detection + affiliate ToS termination make copying a path to bans, not earnings.

4. **Compliance addition:** the make-money-online adjacency requires a hard no-income-claims rule (no "$X/month" promises). Encoded in kb/policies/.

5. **Brand: "SoloStack"** — AI tools and workflows for running a one-person business.

## Earnings model (honest)
YouTube/Pinterest can reach meaningful views in weeks–months (vs 6–12mo for SEO), but earnings still require audience: expect near-zero for the first 1–2 months, then compounding if formats hit. Recurring commissions mean each conversion pays monthly — the compounding is the point.
