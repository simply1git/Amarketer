# Canva Canvassador Program

- **Commissions:** not publicly listed in 2026; historical program paid up to $36 per Canva Pro subscriber. Capture actual terms at application.
- **Application:** submit website/channel, promotion method, audience description. Educator-focused audiences are an explicitly favored category — our niche is a natural fit.
- **Product fit:** Canva for Education is free for K-12 teachers, which limits conversion on teacher content; target the paid-Pro angle (tutors, course creators, homeschool parents) instead.
- **Sequencing:** requires a live site — apply after launch (Phase 3).
