# Amazon Associates

- **Rates (indicative):** ~1–4.5% by category (verify at signup); 24-hour cookie (90 days for items added to cart within 24h).
- **Account survival rule:** 3 qualifying sales in first 180 days or closure — re-applying is allowed, but sequence signup AFTER traffic exists.
- **Social posting:** allowed on Pinterest/YouTube if the account is listed in your Associates profile. Disclosure required on every pin/description; Amazon additionally requires its own disclosure statement on the site.
- **Regional note:** choose the storefront matching the audience we target (traffic will be mostly US on Pinterest → amazon.com program; payout method for non-US residents: gift card/check/bank options vary — confirm at signup).
- **Link hygiene:** no link cloaking/shorteners that hide the amazon.com destination (ToS ban); our `/go/` redirects are same-site and transparent — verify acceptability at signup, else use raw tagged links on hub pages.
