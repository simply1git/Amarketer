# No Income Claims

**Banned:** "make $5k/month with this tool", "quit your job", "passive income guaranteed", earnings screenshots as social proof, thumbnails with dollar figures implying results.

**Allowed:** "this automates your invoicing", "I built a working funnel on the free plan in 40 minutes", factual pricing/commission info, "tools professionals use to run one-person businesses".

**Reframe pattern:** outcome-of-tool, not outcome-of-wallet. ("Saves ~3 hours of editing per video" ✔ / "earn $500 more per week" ✘)
