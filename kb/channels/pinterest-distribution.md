# Pinterest Distribution

## Why this channel
Side-hustle, productivity, and small-business content is a major Pinterest category; distribution is search-like (pins surface for months, unlike feed socials), matching our evergreen content. It delivers traffic in weeks while Google takes months.

## Setup (owner, one-time)
Free business account, claim the website, create 4–6 boards matching content clusters ("free business tools", "solopreneur productivity", "email marketing", "AI tools tested").

## Per-post workflow
1. Derive 3–5 pin concepts from the post: the comparison table, a "which tool for you" decision checklist, a stat callout.
2. 2:3 vertical graphics (1000×1500), large text overlay, consistent brand colors/font.
3. Titles ≤ 100 chars with the post's target keyword; descriptions 200–400 chars, keyword-natural, ending with disclosure tag when affiliate-relevant.
4. Link pins to the post URL (never directly to affiliate links — most programs ban direct Pinterest affiliate deep-linking; check per-program ToS).
5. Stagger publishing (1 pin/day) rather than bulk-posting.

## Measurement
Monthly export of pin analytics (impressions, saves, outbound clicks) → `ops/reports/` → learned entries on formats that work.
